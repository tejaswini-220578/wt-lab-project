About This Project

Farmers often lose money because middlemen buy their crops at low prices and sell them at high margins. This portal connects farmers directly with the government, ensuring fair prices, transparency, and direct payments. By eliminating middlemen, farmers can earn according to their hard work and the government can plan better with accurate crop data.
